History of Instruments

How it started

The history of musical instruments began over 50,000 60,000 years ago, originating from human efforts to mimic natural sounds using bone, wood, and skin for ritualistic, hunting, or celebratory purposes. Early instruments were simple percussion and wind devices, later evolving into complex stringed, brass, and electronic instruments as technologies, social structures, and artistry developed.

Why did we created them

  1. Musical instruments date back over 37,000 years, starting with basic flutes made from animal bones.
  2. Musical instruments often originated from those used for other purposes, like hunting horns becoming the French horn.
